Lead Devops

Profile

Our Lead DevOps Engineer acts as the bridge between development and operations, ensuring our software lifecycle is fast, reliable, and secure. With a focus on architecting scalable infrastructure and fostering a culture of automation, they lead the charge in site reliability and system performance. They don’t just fix pipelines—they design the systems that allow our entire engineering team to ship code with confidence.

Technical Stack & Expertise

This role thrives on a sophisticated blend of cloud-native technologies and automation frameworks. Key areas of mastery include:
-Cloud Infrastructure: Expert-level management of AWS/GCP ecosystems.
-Orchestration: Advanced Kubernetes (K8s) cluster management and Docker containerization.
-Infrastructure as Code (IaC): Building modular, reusable environments using Terraform or Ansible.
-CI/CD Pipelines: Streamlining deployments via GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or Jenkins.

Strategic Leadership

Beyond the terminal, our Lead DevOps Engineer is a strategic mentor. They are responsible for defining SLOs/SLIs, managing cloud budgets through FinOps best practices, and implementing rigorous security protocols (DevSecOps). By mentoring junior engineers and identifying systemic bottlenecks, they ensure our infrastructure isn't just a support tool, but a competitive advantage for the business.
