Cleaning Agent

Profile

The Cleaning Agent is the silent engine behind our professional environment, ensuring our physical workspace is as polished and functional as our digital products. They do more than maintain surfaces; they curate a space that promotes focus, health, and well-being. By managing the details of our surroundings, they create the high-standard atmosphere that allows the rest of the team to perform at their best.

Sanitation & Facility Maintenance

This role requires a meticulous eye for detail and a deep understanding of environmental safety standards. Their expertise includes:
Environmental Sanitization: Implementing rigorous disinfection protocols to maintain a germ-free and healthy workspace for all staff.
Asset Preservation: Utilizing specialized techniques and materials to maintain diverse surfaces, from high-tech flooring to ergonomic furniture.
Inventory Management: Overseeing the procurement and sustainable usage of maintenance supplies and chemical stocks.
Safety Compliance: Ensuring all paths, exits, and common areas meet health and safety regulations through proactive hazard identification.

Operational Reliability

A great Cleaning Agent is a master of logistics and discretion. They operate with high autonomy, managing complex schedules to ensure maintenance happens seamlessly without disrupting the workflow of the engineering or creative teams. By identifying facility needs before they become problems—such as reporting minor repairs or optimizing waste management—they serve as the frontline of our building’s longevity and operational excellence.
